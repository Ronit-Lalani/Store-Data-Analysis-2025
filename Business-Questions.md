## ❓ Business Questions

The following key questions were defined before analysis to guide dashboard creation and insights generation:

1. What is the overall sales performance (Total Revenue, Orders, AOV)?
2. Which product categories generate the most sales?
3. Which sales channels perform the best?
4. Who are the main customers (Gender-based analysis)?
5. Which states generate the highest revenue?
6. How do sales trends change over time (monthly analysis)?
7. What is the average order value across different segments?
8. Which combinations (Category, Channel, Gender) drive the most sales?
